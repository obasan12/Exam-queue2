    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I register for the exam?

    To register for the exam, click on the "Register for Exam" button and enter your matriculation number and email address. The system will verify your details and assign you an exam slot.

    What should I bring to the exam?

    You should bring your university ID card, exam token (which you will receive after registration), and any other materials specified for your particular exam.

    Can I reschedule my exam?

    Rescheduling is only allowed in emergency situations. Please contact the exam administrator if you need to reschedule.

    How do I check my exam status?

    You can check your exam status by clicking on the "Check Exam Status" button and entering your matriculation number and token ID.
